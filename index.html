'Eilenriede'

One of Europe's most important urban forests in the heart of Hanover has a lot to offer. With around 640 hectares, the Eilenriede is one of the largest forests in Europe in the heart of a major city - even Hyde Park in London can't compete.

'Maschsee'

Maritime paradise in the middle of Hanover - the Maschsee is one of the most popular destinations and recreational areas in Hanover and attracts thousands of visitors every year, especially during the Maschsee Festival.

'Herrenhäuser Gardens'

Enchanting flower beds, fireworks competition or the giant water fountain. This and much more makes a visit to the Herrenhäuser Gardens worthwhile.
In addition, barbecue can be held near the canal

Worldwide unique arched elevator

Climb up city hall dome with arched elevator and enjoy the impressive view over Hanover.
On your way up, you are first transported vertically and then upward at an angle of 17 degrees. A window in the roof of the cabin allows you to follow the ride up close. Another window in the floor of the cabin can be switched to transparent at the push of a button -
this promises a special thrill.
